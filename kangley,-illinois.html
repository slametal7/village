Kangley, Illinois

Kangley is a village in LaSalle County, Illinois, United States. The population was 235 at the 2020 census, down from 251 at the 2010 census. It is part of the Ottawa Micropolitan Statistical Area. It is a part of the geographic region known as Streatorland.





== Geography ==

Kangley occupies a bluff on the west side of the Vermilion River, which flows northwest to the Illinois River at LaSalle. It is 3 miles (5 km) northwest of the city of Streator.

According to the 2010 census, Kangley has a total area of 0.26 square miles (0.67 km2), all land.





== Demographics ==



As of the 2020 census there were 235 people, 120 households, and 79 families residing in the village. The population density was 900.38 inhabitants per square mile (347.64/km2). There were 106 housing units at an average density of 406.13 per square mile (156.81/km2).
