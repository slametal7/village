America Alludu

America Alludu (transl. American son-in-law) is a 1985 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by K. Vasu and starring Suman and Bhanupriya.





== Plot ==

Raju, a doctor in Chicago, marries his maradalu (transl. cross cousin) Radha from the village. However, Radha is unable to adjust to the western lifestyle and frequently makes Raju uncomfortable. Due to a mistake made by Radha, a patient dies in Raju's hospital. With this, Raju leaves Radha and goes back to India. Radha single handedly takes care of their child. How Radha wins back Raju's heart forms the rest of the story.
